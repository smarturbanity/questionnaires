How we use your data

SmartUrbanity is a public multi-partner research initiative. Participation is voluntary and supports inclusive urban accessibility planning.

What we collect

  • Optional email/access metadata
  • Survey responses (profile, behaviour, perceptions)
  • Optional geolocated reports on safety/infrastructure
  • Basic technical metadata (device/session/timestamps)

Why we collect data

Data support scientific research, accessibility analysis, and planning indicators.

Only anonymised aggregate outputs are made public; controlled pseudonymised sharing may occur with qualified partners under formal agreements.

Never used for commercial purposes.

How your data are protected

  • Identifier data are stored separately and encrypted
  • Research records use internal pseudonymous IDs
  • Published outputs are aggregated and anonymised

Current scope

This repository version covers surveys and spatial reports. GPS trip-trace storage is excluded.
